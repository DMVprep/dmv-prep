Frequently asked questions

What is the difference between Pass and Premium?

Pass is a one-time payment that fully unlocks one state forever. Premium is a subscription that unlocks all 50 states, progress tracking, and multilingual translations. If you only need to pass the test in one state, Pass is the best value.

Can I upgrade from Pass to Premium later?

Yes. If you already purchased Pass and decide you want all 50 states, you can upgrade to Premium at any time.

Will I be charged automatically?

Only for Premium subscriptions. Pass is a one-time charge with no recurring billing. Premium renews monthly or annually until you cancel.

Can I cancel my Premium subscription?

Yes, anytime. No questions asked. You keep access until the end of your billing period.

No. We are an independent study tool. Our questions are based on official handbooks but we are not affiliated with any government agency.
